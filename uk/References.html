Info

2Bona, 2 Pendants, 2 Materials, 2 Colors, each piece of the pair is unique on its own in terms of materials and techniques, used to make them to represent the record label: Gorilla in the most unique way possible and to give it a very powerful look.

Client:

Kun

Material:

Unique white bronze alloy

Stones:

150 6A Quality CZ white/yellow

Story:

To make this pair of jewelry unique, each pendant of the pair was made in its own unique bronze alloy to give it a unique color and shine to it, which make the individual color of the stones used for the pieces really pop out. A lot of small details was given to the pieces, for example the names of the artist’s on the forehead of the gorilla to make it appear like a tattoo and the arrangement of the stones in the crown for a more 3D appearance.

Info

New logo new piece of jewelry, hard work has to be rewarded one day was here the motivation behind this unique piece of jewelry. Combining the important things out of the life of the Client: himself, like one of his favorite comic series written on the back of the piece and the hard work of his DJ and music business, in form of his logo in the style of the one he has on his Instagram together makes not only a piece of jewelry, but a sentimental piece of art.

DJ Fricktion

Material:

Light yellow aluminum / silver bronze

Story:

Special attention was given to make the logo itself appear as authentic and original as possible. Together with the given background texture it makes the already 3D engraved logo pop even more out. No stones was used to highlight only the important things of the piece itself.

Info

A unique representation of the logo and work of the Client: itself. The date of the beginning of the YouTube career of the Client: and the logo itself fused together in a modern and somewhat digital look.

Client:

Nedim Lepić

Material:

Bright white silver aluminum bronze

Stones:

69 6A london blue / black CZ

Story:

The logo itself was the main thing beside all other details, a lot of attention was given, to give it a authentic and at the same time unique appearance, by giving it a rough surface. The date which marks the beginning of the YouTube career of the Client:, was made to look like a box almost like a digital clock. The background of the logo itself, was made in a way that it look like a paper texture. Together with the frame around in blue and the four black colored stones on each corner, the logo and the background, it will appear all together like a art picture as a piece of jewelry.
